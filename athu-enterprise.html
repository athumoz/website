AT Circular 08/2025OCDE Guidelines+2 sources
Evidence-based compliance summary
Transfer pricing rules in Mozambique are governed by the General Tax Code (CIRT) and AT Circular 08/2025. Multinationals with annual intercompany transactions exceeding MZN 50 million must prepare contemporaneous transfer pricing documentation using OECD-consistent methodologies. Filing deadline is 90 days after fiscal year end.
